ABOUT US

Padhai is an innovative learning organization created to improve the quality of education in India. Established in 2004,when two friends could not handle the sight of young kids working and living in despair ,hence decided to give back to society by nurturing young minds and providing a path for them to grow and live a happy and fruitful life. Started to provide education to children in the slums of Mumbai, Padhai has grown in both scope and scale, with programs today reaching children and youth across the country.Padhai Foundation works as a catalyst in the cycle of change, complementing and supplementing government efforts to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals. We sensitize and partner with like-minded institutions and individuals to implement high-impact programmes.

Padhai focuses on high-quality, low-cost and replicable interventions to address gaps in the education system. Working directly with children and youth as well as through large-scale collaborations with government systems, Padhai programs reach millions of lives every year.

STORIES IN MOTION

MEET BHAVNA

Bhavna could not complete her education as her father did not believe in educating girls. A lot of arguments and fights within her family regarding her education made her father to remove Bhavna, her siblings and her mother out of the house. Being all broken with the situation, Padhai foundation was a stepping stone in Bhavna's life. She continued her education under 'She can be Anything' campaign. On completing her studies, she wished to help all those other children like her and hence she is now a teacher in one of the Padhai foundation centers.
